Our Story

మా కథ

Our story begins at Sunrise Estate, where fine Robusta has thrived since 1952. Nestled in the misty slopes of the Western Ghats, the estate grows coffee under wild-born trees and along mountain streams.

In 2018, the estate found its new parents in Vasu and Vani Polavarapu, a couple whose profound love for farming led them to transform Sunrise Estate into a home to grow and process the finest Robusta.

Western Ghats, Karnataka
Bold, resilient — the sustainable future of coffee cultivation.

True to its name, Robusta stands as the industry's vital alternative as climate change challenges traditional methods. Hardy, expressive, and deeply rooted in volcanic soil — each cup carries the memory of morning mist and ancient trees.
